Pressure Cooker Lentils

A pressure cooker makes preparing lentils quick and easy!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups lentils
  • 6 cups water

Steps

  1. Wash lentils
  2. Add lentils and water to pressure cooker
  3. Lock cooker and cook on high until top begins to rock
  4. As soon as top begins to rock set a timer for 4 minutes
  5. Turn heat down until top is just gently rocking
  6. After 4 minutes, remove cooker from stove and let it cool on its own until safe to open
