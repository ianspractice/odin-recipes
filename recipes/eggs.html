Steamed Hardboiled Eggs

Steaming is a great way to make hardboiled eggs!

Ingredients/Tools

  • 4 eggs
  • small pot with lid
  • steamer that fits in the pot
  • water

Directions

  1. Place steamer basket in pot and fill with enough water to come just below the basket
  2. Cover pot and bring to a boil
  3. Gently place eggs in basket
  4. Cover and cook for 9 minutes
  5. Transfer eggs to ice water and let sit for 5 minutes
  6. Peel and enjoy!
